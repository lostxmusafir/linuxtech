Essay: Dream – A Person with Big Dreams

Dream was a person who always believed in success. Dream had one clear goal in life, and Dream worked hard every day to achieve that goal. Dream believed that one strong dream can change one life completely.

Every morning, Dream woke up with motivation. Dream studied, learned new skills, and improved one step at a time. Dream knew that one day hard work would bring success. Many people around Dream saw how focused Dream was.

One day, Dream helped one friend who was feeling sad. Dream told that one positive thought can change everything. After hearing Dream’s words, that friend felt better. Dream always tried to make one good impact on people.

Dream also believed that one mistake is not the end. Dream learned from every mistake and moved forward. Because of this attitude, Dream became stronger every day. Many people started respecting Dream.

In the end, Dream proved that one person with one dream and one strong mindset can achieve great success. Dream’s story teaches us that if one person believes in a dream, then that dream can become reality.
